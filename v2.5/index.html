ABOUT

Usva Films is a wildlife filmmaking company from Finland working primarily in the nordics and baltics. The production company was officially started in 2020 but our work has been going on for over a decade. Usva Films is well connected and has close relations to almost all professional wildlife filmmakers and wildlife scouting and hide services in Finland, Sweden and Estonia. Our main product is sequence work for international productions in the nordics and baltics. We also fix, scout and assist. Working with the industry standard equipment and with the best contacts one can find in the nordics, Usva films offers a one stop shop for large scale productions searching for stories from the arctic north.
